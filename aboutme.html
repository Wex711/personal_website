About Me

As you already know, my name is Wexler. I also go by Wex. I am from Lexington Kentucky and have lived here for all of my life, but plan to move after graduation. Some of my favorite places are my local park and the arboretum off of UK's campus.

I have two dogs, Stuart, who is a Maltese, and Kelly, who is a Chihuahua. I also have two sisters and live with my dad. My favorite foods are portobello mushrooms, soft serve ice cream, Panera baguettes, and vegetable tika masala.

My favorite movies are Stand by Me, Shawshank Redemption, and Brokeback Mountain. My favorite franchises are Pokemon and Minecraft. I enjoy playing the games these series have to offer, as well as Terraria and modded Minecraft.
